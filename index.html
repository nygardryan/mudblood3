MUD & BLOOD

An HTML5 tribute to the classic Flash game

France, 1944. Your sector. Germans are coming from the north, and they will not stop coming.

  • Earn Tactical Points (TP) from kills. Spend them on men, defenses and fire support.
  • Deploy your men in the lower half of the field. Mortar & artillery can hit anywhere.
  • Fire support is dangerous to your own men. So it was in real life.
  • If 7 Germans slip past the bottom of your screen, your line collapses. Game over.
  • There is no winning. There is only lasting one more wave.
